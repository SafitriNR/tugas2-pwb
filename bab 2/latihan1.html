Common Food of the French Quarter
You can visit New Orleans and have a great time without ever leaving the picturesque and partyin' French Quarter area, particulary if you partake of some of these fabulous local food:
  • Beignets small deep fried donuts in powdered sugar. Best with a streaming fresh cup of coffee
  • Seafood Gumboa stewlike soup that's delicius. Typically served with a side of white rice that's best dumped into the soup directly. Skip the chicken gumbo some places serve too : the seafood is definitely better!
  • Jambalayathe best of all possible dinner. You'll just have to order it so you can find out what it's about
  • Alcoholit's the grease on the wheels of the tourist experience in the French Quarter, but i'm not convinced it's as necessary for a good time as the bars suggest ...
Whatever you do, make sure you have F U N!
